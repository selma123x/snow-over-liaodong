## FORD  (16s)
THE FORD — M1, the raid (Seol 11). FILMED: 3 live shots, captions OFF in-game.
Camera staging: slow orbital descent to the river edge, ending close and low

- **0.5s** — He catches them at the ford. One man, on foot, against seven.
- **3.5s** — You watch your father take them apart — the first with an arrow, the last with his hands.
- **7.0s** — Then his arms are around you, and he is saying your name into your hair —
- **10.0s** — — and his arms go slack. You feel it before you understand it.
- **13.0s** — A blade behind you is a funeral. He was holding you when he stopped listening.
